ABOUT US
WiRun

WiRun is a nonprofit organization dedicated to creating a safe and supportive running community that motivates individuals to achieve their fitness goals. Founded by Lesedi Onna in 2001,
WiRun started with small 5K (3.1 miles) runs and, thanks to the unwavering support of the community, has grown to host full 42K marathons and 50K ultramarathons.

Raised in an environment where unhealthy food choices and sedentary lifestyles were the norm, Lesedi Onna found herself trapped in a cycle of poor health. Running became her lifeline,
transformed her life in ways she never imagined. Driven by her personal journey, Lesedi founded WiRun with a mission to make running accessible and enjoyable for everyone, providing a
healthy environment where people can thrive.

Why Running?

Running offers numerous health benefits, including:
- Improved Cardiovascular Health: Reduces the risk of heart disease.
- Weight Management:Burns calories effectively, aiding in weight control.
- Increased Stamina: Builds endurance and energy levels.
- Enhanced Bone Density: Strengthens bones, reducing the risk of osteoporosis.
- Body Toning: Engages multiple muscle groups for a lean, toned physique.
-Discipline and Stress Relief: Running fosters discipline and releases endorphins,
providing a natural stress reliever and a sense of accomplishment.

Our Mission

WiRun aims to build a strong community where runners, from beginners to competitive athletes, can thrive. We provide athletes with the facilities and support they need to excel, whether they aim
to represent South Africa on the world stage or simply improve their personal bests. WiRun organizes marathons in every South African province and is expanding to more cities, spreading our
message of health and well-being.

Community Impact

WiRun isn’t just about running. We fund medical professionals to speak at schools, educating students about healthy physical, mental, and hygienic habits. We also partner with brands to offer
winners sponsorships, scholarships, and free athletic gear, ensuring they stay on a healthy path. Additionally, winners are featured in magazines, and their social media profiles are promoted to
support their fitness careers, along with cash prizes.

Join Us

WiRun is more than a race; it’s a movement towards a healthier, more active life. Join us and be part of a community that believes in the power of running to change lives.
